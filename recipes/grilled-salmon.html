Grilled Salmon

A delicious salmon fillet

This grilled salmon tastes so good! A simple soy sauce and brown sugar marinade, with hints of lemon and garlic, are the perfect salty-sweet complement to rich salmon fillets. Even my 9-year-old loves this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Lemon pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Water
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets
  2. Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat
  4. Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork
